About URC

The University Rover Challenge (URC) is the world's premier robotics competition for college students. Teams design and build Mars rovers to compete in field tests that simulate real Mars exploration missions. URC challenges students to solve real-world engineering problems and pushes the boundaries of what's possible in robotics.
