The rover is designed to tackle the most challenging terrains and complex missions, including autonomous exploration, sample collection, and equipment servicing. In the University Rover Challenge (URC) 2024, the UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) from United International University (UIU) achieved remarkable success.
