UIU Mars Rover Team Achievements

2022

  • Successfully designed and built the team's first functional Mars rover prototype.
  • Won the Best Rookie Team Award at a national robotics competition.
  • Partnered with local engineering firms for materials and components.

2023

  • Developed an autonomous navigation system using AI and computer vision.
  • Participated in the University Rover Challenge (URC) and achieved top rankings.
  • Published a research paper on autonomous navigation for Mars rovers.

2024

  • Competed in the European Rover Challenge (ERC) and secured a top 10 position.
  • Introduced a modular design and solar-powered energy system for the rover.
  • Conducted community outreach programs to inspire young students in STEM.

About UMRT

The UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) is a group of passionate students dedicated to designing and building the next generation of Mars and Lunar rovers. We aim to push the boundaries of space exploration and innovation.
