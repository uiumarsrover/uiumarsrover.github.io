AXIOS

Motors

Brushless Geared Motors powers AXIOS to drive through extreme challenges.

Steering Mechanism

High Torque BLDC Motor Driven Actuators with weight distribution techniques made the steering mechanism of AXIOS better then before.

Wheels

3D Printed Custom Design wheels enables AXIOS to manuever over extreme terrains.

Primary Manipulator Arm

Handles payload upto 15 kg and can perform precision tasks. Operated by burshless motor driven cycloidal actuators with a parallel sliding end-effector.

Chassis

Made out of Carbon-fiber, made AXIOS rigid and stable yet lightweight to withstand extreme conditions.

Rover Communication Link

Long Range 915Mhz Communication System enables telemetry and remote control over 2.5 KM.

Video Transmission Link

High Power 5.8 Ghz Video Transmission Link allows AXIOS to transmit encrypted low-latency uninturrupted video feed to over 2.5 KM.

AXIOS, The worthy of all


AXIOS is our 4th Generation Rover. The upgraded carbon fiber chassis, paired with newly optimized 3D-printed wheels, delivers optimal suspension and traction, enabling AXIOS to navigate demanding terrain with precision and reliability.
