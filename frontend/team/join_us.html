UIU Mars Rover Team Recruitment 2025

Are you enthusiastic about Robotics, Space exploration, Research, and the search for life beyond Earth? This is the perfect opportunity for you! We are seeking dedicated individuals who are enthusiastic about contributing to the continuous development of our rover. We are excited to build a new core team for upcoming events such as the University Rover Challenge 2025, Anatolian Rover Challenge 2025, and European Rover Challenge 2025. We welcome applications from students of all departments at UIU.

We are looking for members with the following skills:

  • Science Sub-Team: Students from Pharmacy, Biotechnology, and Genetic Engineering are encouraged to apply.
  • Mechanical Sub-Team: Knowledge of SolidWorks, Fusion 360, and mechanical fabrication.
  • Electrical Sub-Team: Experience in circuit design, microcontrollers, and electronics.
  • Software & Autonomous Sub-Team: Skills in image processing, machine learning, and technical writing.
  • Aerial & Communication Sub-Team: Experience with drones, networking, and communication protocols.
  • Media and Public Relations Sub-Team: Content creation, graphics design, and communication skills.
  • Logistics: BBA students with organizational and management skills are encouraged to apply.
