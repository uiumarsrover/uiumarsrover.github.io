ABOUT US

The UIU Mars Rover Team of United International University (UIU) has been working since August 2, 2021, towards developing the rover “AXIOS” to participate in the University Rover Challenge (URC) 2025, under the supervision of CAIR (Center for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics). The team is proceeding with the goal of igniting the interest in robotics in enthusiastic students from all over the country.
