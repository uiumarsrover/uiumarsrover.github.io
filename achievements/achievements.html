Affiliated with the University

July 2021

The UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) project was launched as a student-driven initiative at United International University (UIU) to compete in the University Rover Challenge (URC) and other international robotics competitions. It began with a group of passionate engineering and robotics enthusiasts working under the guidance of faculty members from UIU's Engineering and CSE departments. Through continuous innovation in mechanical design, AI-based vision, autonomous navigation, and robotic arms, the team rapidly gained recognition. United International University started to fund the UIU Mars Rover Team for further Developement.
