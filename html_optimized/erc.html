About ERC

The European Rover Challenge (ERC) is an annual international robotics competition held in Poland that brings together the best university teams from around the world. ERC features Marsyard tasks that closely mimic real NASA/ESA missions, including Probing and Presentation challenges that test teams' engineering capabilities and scientific approach.
