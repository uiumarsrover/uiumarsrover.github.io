About ARC

The Anatolian Rover Challenge (ARC) is a prestigious international robotics competition that focuses on lunar exploration and space robotics. Teams from around the world compete in tasks that simulate lunar surface operations and astronaut assistance, pushing the boundaries of autonomous robotics and space technology.
