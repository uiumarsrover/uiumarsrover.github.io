UIU Mars Rover Team Recruitment 2025

Are you enthusiastic about Robotics, Space exploration, Research, and the search for life beyond Earth? This is the perfect opportunity for you! We are seeking dedicated individuals who are enthusiastic about contributing to the continuous development of our rover. We are excited to build a new core team for upcoming events such as the University Rover Challenge 2025, Anatolian Rover Challenge 2025, and European Rover Challenge 2025. We welcome applications from students of all departments at UIU.

The UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) is a group of passionate students dedicated to designing and building the next generation of Mars and Lunar rovers. We aim to push the boundaries of space exploration and innovation.
