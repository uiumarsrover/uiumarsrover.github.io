OUR VISION

The UIU Mars Rover Team, consisting of over 25 members from diverse educational backgrounds, is dedicated to advancing space robotics research in Bangladesh. Their mission is to develop high-quality robotic systems and showcase them in esteemed international competitions.

This commitment has led them to secure first place among Asian teams at the University Rover Challenge (URC) for three consecutive years‚Äî2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025.

Their consistent excellence underscores their dedication to elevating Bangladesh's presence in the global space robotics arena.
