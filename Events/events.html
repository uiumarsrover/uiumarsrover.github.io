Space Innovation Camp Bangladesh

The UIU Mars Rover Team had the joy of hosting the Astro Robotics Team from Space Innovation Camp Bangladesh. We were honored to have with us Prof. Emeritus Dr. M. Rezwan Khan, Director of the Institute for Advanced Research, Dr. Hasan Sarwar, Dean of the School of Science and Engineering, and Mr. Ariful Hasan Opu, President of Space Innovation Camp Bangladesh, who inspired the young participants with their valuable words.

The Astro Robotics Team explored our lab, learned about our journey in building world-class rovers, and engaged in an exciting Q&A session. Our mentor, Md Abid Hossain, shared the story of our team along with other members, encouraging the students to dream bigger in robotics and space exploration.

Their curiosity, energy, and passion for innovation reminded us why the future of space exploration in Bangladesh is so promising. We are proud to share our story with the next generation of innovators and look forward to seeing where their journey takes them!

ArcaNoca 251 Workshop

UIU Robotics & UIU Mars Rover Team collaborated for an engaging and exciting IoT & Robotics Workshop "ArcaNoca 251" for all the student members of UIU Robotics. This workshop's goal was to kickstart students' journey into robotics and IoT hardware, and we were excited to demonstrate our rover AXIOS at the workshop. It was a fun and exciting day. Bangladesh Space Olympiad 2025 – Final Round at AIUB

The Final Round of the Bangladesh Space Olympiad 2025 at AIUB was not just about competition, but about connection. Members of GUB Mars Rover Team, BRACU Mongol Tori, MIST Mongol Barota, and UIU Mars Rover Team came together for a group photo that symbolizes something greater — a growing community of passionate innovators dedicated to advancing space exploration in Bangladesh.

Together, we are not just building rovers; we are building a community driven by knowledge, collaboration, and a shared vision for the future.
